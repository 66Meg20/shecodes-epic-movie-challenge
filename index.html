story: La Danse Macabre STORY The world has been split in two. The Ark, which floats in the sky, radiating light, and the Surface, a wasteland whose inhabitants fight for what little scraps they have left. Libel, the leader of Rebellion - one of the Surface’s many resistance groups - has a sudden epiphany for changing the world: he must abduct the Celestial who only appears to their subjects during a festival, held once every four years. As a symbol of the Church of Nerve, the Celestial is also the object of worship to all denizensof the Ark. With them gone, neither the Ark, the Church of Nerve, nor the Surface can remain separate. Libel believes all parties will then engage in dialogue that will bring them closer to true freedom and equality.
